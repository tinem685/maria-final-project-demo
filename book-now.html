Welcome to My Author Page

I’m Maria Tineo, an eighteen-year-old bilingual journalist with a passion for storytelling and a drive to uncover the narratives that often go unnoticed. As a student at The New School’s Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts, I’m honing my skills in journalism and design, fueled by my commitment to making a difference through meaningful stories.

About Me

My journey into journalism is rooted in my dedication to amplifying voices and advocating for causes that matter. Whether it’s crafting compelling articles or collaborating with peers, I thrive on exploring stories that inspire change and evoke empathy. From managing newsroom deadlines to delving into diverse perspectives, I embrace every challenge as an opportunity to grow as a journalist and storyteller.

Future Aspirations

I aim to further my work by sharing the experiences of individuals whose voices deserve recognition. My goal is to traverse the streets of the world, uncovering hidden truths and telling stories that resonate deeply with audiences everywhere.
